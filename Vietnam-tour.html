Vietnam Tour

7 Days • $899

Discover Vietnam’s breathtaking landscapes, vibrant cities, and rich culture. From Halong Bay cruises to local street food experiences, enjoy a journey full of unforgettable memories.

📍 Location: Asia
⭐ Rating: 4.8
👥 Group: 10 People
🌤 Best Season: Nov - Apr

Trip Itinerary

Your 7-day journey plan

Day 1 - Arrival in Hanoi

Welcome to Vietnam. Check into your hotel, relax after travel, and enjoy a peaceful evening exploring nearby streets and local surroundings.

Day 2 - Hanoi City Tour

Discover Hanoi’s rich culture by visiting temples, markets, and historic landmarks while enjoying authentic Vietnamese street food and vibrant city life.

Day 3 - Halong Bay Cruise

Travel to Halong Bay and board a scenic cruise, surrounded by limestone islands, calm waters, and breathtaking natural beauty throughout the journey.

Day 4 - Island Activities

Enjoy exciting island activities like kayaking, swimming, and relaxing on beaches while experiencing the serene atmosphere and stunning coastal landscapes.

Day 5 - Local Village Visit

Visit a traditional village, interact with locals, learn cultural practices, and experience authentic Vietnamese lifestyle and warm hospitality firsthand.

Day 6 - Free Day

Spend a free day exploring the city, shopping for souvenirs, enjoying local cuisine, or relaxing at your hotel with optional activities available.

Day 7 - Departure

Check out from the hotel, transfer to the airport, and depart with unforgettable memories of your Vietnam journey and wonderful travel experiences.
