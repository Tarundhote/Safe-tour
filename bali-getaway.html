Trip Itinerary

Your 5-day journey plan

Day 1 – Arrival in Bali

Welcome to Bali! Arrive at Ngurah Rai Airport, transfer to your hotel, and relax. In the evening, enjoy a peaceful beachside sunset and explore nearby local markets or cafes.

Day 2 – Beaches & Temple Tour

Visit famous beaches like Kuta and Seminyak, perfect for relaxing or surfing. Later, explore Tanah Lot and Uluwatu Temple while enjoying breathtaking ocean views and sunset scenery.

Day 3 – Ubud & Nature Experience

Travel to Ubud and discover lush rice terraces, waterfalls, and the Sacred Monkey Forest. Experience Bali’s natural beauty and try the famous jungle swing for stunning views.

Day 4 – Island Adventure

Enjoy an exciting island trip to Nusa Penida. Visit crystal-clear beaches, dramatic cliffs, and enjoy activities like snorkeling, swimming, and capturing scenic photos.

Day 5 – Leisure & Departure

Spend your last day shopping for souvenirs, relaxing at a spa, or enjoying a café. Later, transfer to the airport with unforgettable memories of your Bali getaway.
